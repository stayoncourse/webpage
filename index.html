Welcome to Stay On Course

A personalized graduation tracker built by high school students, for high school students.

The Problem

A lack of concrete planning at the high school level can not only impact student performance, but also a student’s ability to fulfill all of their graduation requirements.

Stay On Course is a visual tool that helps students easily access their graduation requirements right from their pockets, helping them to plan ahead, stay organized, and be successful.

The Mission

Stay On Course guides students through the process of creating a long-term plan for their high school education.

Students can select which diploma they intend to graduate with, and can use the app’s personalized dashboard to plan ahead and fulfill each requirement associated with that diploma.

Features

The Coursework tab allows the student to view their previously entered courses by year taken, as well as their cumulative and semester GPA.

The Volunteering tab displays community service hours. It will alert students how many hours you have already completed, both on and off campus.

The Activities tab tracks clubs, organizations, and sports, both inside and outside of school.

On the Dashboard, students can view personalized messages and feedback based on their progress, complete with a summary of community service and extracurricular activities, as well as a credit tracker by subject area.
